Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

🔒

Your privacy is protected

Pictimer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your device.

Data Collection

Pictimer does not collect any data from its users. The app was designed with privacy as a core principle.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No third-party services have access to your data

On-Device Processing

All work Pictimer does happens entirely on your device. The photo timestamps you edit stay on your device and are never transmitted elsewhere.

The app reads and writes photo creation dates using Apple's Photos framework, only with your explicit permission. This data is processed locally and is never stored outside of the standard system frameworks.

Apple Photos

Pictimer integrates with Apple Photos to read and modify photo timestamps. This integration is governed by Apple's strict privacy guidelines:

  • You control which photos the app can access
  • Your library is encrypted and protected by iOS, iPadOS and macOS
  • Pictimer only reads and writes timestamps; it never copies or exports your photos
  • Access can be revoked at any time in Settings

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. The commitment to not collecting user data will remain unchanged.
